Our 2024 Featured Speakers

Dr. Maya Thompson

Chief Artificial Intelligence Scientist at Quantum Dynamics Inc.

Dr. Maya Thompson leads a team of researchers at Quantum Dynamics Inc., where she specializes in the development of cutting-edge artificial intelligence algorithms. At TechPulse Expo, Dr. Thompson will discuss the future of AI and its transformative potential across industries, focusing on the ethical implications and opportunities for innovation in the AI landscape.

Sarah Alvarez

CEO and Co-Founder of BioTech Innovations

As the CEO and Co-Founder of BioTech Innovations, Sarah Alvarez is at the forefront of revolutionizing healthcare through biotechnology. At TechPulse Expo, Sarah will share insights into the latest breakthroughs in biotech, including advancements in personalized medicine and the role of genomics in shaping the future of healthcare.

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

Space Systems Engineer at StellarTech Solutions

Dr. Elena Rodriguez is a Space Systems Engineer at StellarTech Solutions, where she specializes in developing next-generation satellite technology. At TechPulse Expo, Dr. Rodriguez will discuss the latest trends in space exploration, highlighting the role of satellite technology in addressing global challenges such as climate change and disaster management.

Michael Johnson

Chief Data Scientist at DataTech Labs

Michael Johnson is the Chief Data Scientist at DataTech Labs, where he leads a team of data experts in harnessing the power of big data for actionable insights. At TechPulse Expo, Michael will delve into the world of data analytics, exploring how businesses can leverage data-driven strategies to drive innovation, enhance decision-making, and gain a competitive edge in today's digital landscape.
